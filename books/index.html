Books I finished in 2017

(*If you have an ad blocker, you won't be able to see the Amazon links to the books.)

Rework

This book is one of my favorites so far this year. It feels like it was written for a person with ADD. Everything is broken out into small nuggets of information and advice. So easy to read with some great content. I will be reading it again in the near future...and checking out their products. Loved it.

The Fred Factor

Mark Sanborn wrote this book based on his personal postal carrier - Fred. The book relates the principles gleaned from the way Fred did his ordinary job in an extrodinary way. This book also comes highly recommended by John Maxwell, so you know it can't be bad. :-)

To Kill a Mockingbird

This book is my daughters favorite book, so I told her I'd read it. It was quite obvious why it is a classic. Hat's off to the late, great, Harper Lee.

What If?: Serious Scientific Answers to Absurd Hypothetical Questions

I thoroughly enjoyed this book. While the content chosen to be in the book is very entertaining and informational, some of the questions that weren't chosen are also shown and illustrated and very funny.
